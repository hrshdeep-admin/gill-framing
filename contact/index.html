Our Journey

2005

The Beginning

Gill Framing was founded with a simple mission: to provide reliable, high-quality framing services to the Alberta construction market. Starting with small residential projects, we quickly built a reputation for excellence.

2010

Growth & Expansion

As demand for our services grew, we expanded our team and took on larger commercial projects. Our commitment to quality and on-time delivery made us a preferred contractor for builders throughout the region.

2015

Industry Recognition

Gill Framing earned multiple industry certifications and awards for safety and quality. We invested in advanced training for our crew and modern equipment to stay ahead of industry standards.

2020
